## What is this study about?

We study how people mentally simulate folding a paper strip.  You will look
at pictures of paper strips and predict what happens when one fold changes.
There are no right or wrong answers — we want to know what feels natural to
you.

## Who can take part?

You can take part if you:

- are 18 years old or older,
- are a native or near-native English speaker, and
- use a desktop or laptop computer (not a phone or tablet).

We expect to recruit about 120 people through Prolific.

## What will I do?

The study takes about 20 minutes.  You will:

1. Read a short intro and give consent.
2. Answer a few questions about yourself (age group, handedness).
3. Complete two short questionnaires on everyday thinking (about 5 minutes).
4. Do the main task: look at paper strip pictures and click your prediction
   (about 12 minutes, 36 short trials).
5. Read a debrief page that explains the full study purpose.

## Will I be paid?

Yes.  Prolific will pay you the amount shown on the study listing.  Payment
is not affected by how you answer.

## What data do we collect?

We collect:

- Your clicks and response times during the task.
- Your questionnaire answers.
- Your browser type and screen size (not your IP address or name).

We do **not** collect your name, email, or IP address.  Your Prolific ID is
converted into a random code before it reaches our server, so we cannot
link your data back to you.

## Where is my data stored?

Data are stored on secure servers in the EU (Ireland).  Only the research
team at [YOUR_INSTITUTION] can access the raw data.  We keep data for up to
10 years.

## Can I delete my data?

Yes.  Email [PI_EMAIL] with the subject "Data deletion — KineMind" within 30
days of completing the study.  We will delete your records within 14 days.
After 30 days, your data are part of the anonymised dataset and cannot be
identified or deleted individually.

## What are the risks?

The study involves no physical risk.  You might find some questions about
thinking styles slightly personal; you can skip any you do not want to
answer.

## Is participation voluntary?

Yes.  You may stop at any time without giving a reason.  You will receive
pro-rated payment for the time you spent.
